Crafty Grandmas' hotel

Welcome to the Crafty Grandmas' hotel

We're the place where every lady can relax and do any crafts she likes. Supplies, cooking and cleaning up is on us! Enjoy your favorite crafting or try something new. Our awesome crafty grandmoms will support you with everything. Just let your talantes bloom!

Our mission

We believe that creativity is an important and necessary part of our lives. We would like every woman, who's busy with her job and/or taking care of her family, had a chance to relax and create. And let us take care of you like a loving grandma would do.

About us

We're two lifetime friends, named Sun and River. That's exactly what our names mean in our native languages. As long as we remember ourselves we love drawing, embroidery, knitting, makrame, gardening, wood works, etc. Crafting helped us stay postive in hard times and brightened our best days. And yes, technically we're grandmoms. River has a gift grandson, and Sun is thinking herself as a grandma of her cousins' grandkids.
